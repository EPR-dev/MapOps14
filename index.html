Who MapOps Is For

If any of this sounds like you, a territory map will change how your team plans and executes.

Teams Where Territories Actually Matter

5–150 reps working regions, routes, or pods where coverage and travel time matter.

  • Uneven books and patchy coverage.
  • Reps drowning in some markets, coasting in others.
  • Leaders guessing where to hire next.

Teams Designing GTM From Scratch

Early-stage teams choosing regions, defining pods, and building a clean, data-informed GTM motion from day one.

  • Identifying high-signal early markets.
  • Designing first-pass territories with real data.
  • Guiding early hiring, pods, and coverage strategy.

Ops & Leaders Who Want Spatial Truth

You are tired of arguing about territories in spreadsheets and Slack threads.

  • Need cleaner QBRs and a clearer GTM story.
  • Planning expansion, consolidation, or a new motion.
  • Know there is opportunity you are not seeing yet.

Who MapOps Is Not For

Honest filter, because MapOps is not for everyone.

  • Fewer than 20 named accounts and no territory motion.
  • 100 percent inbound with no field or outbound component.
  • Orgs that will not enforce territories in practice.

When territories matter, MapOps matters. When they do not, it is probably overkill.
